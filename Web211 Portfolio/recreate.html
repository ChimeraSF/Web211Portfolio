Photo Recreate

The assignment was to recreate an image using vector tools. Original image on the left, recreate on the right. Using a combination of the pen, pencil, and the direct selection tools, I carefully traced and adjusted my flower render. I started first with a base, then added in the yellow edges, shading, and lighting. I ran out of time to do the full image, so I focused on the flowers and gave the background image a crosshatch filter.
