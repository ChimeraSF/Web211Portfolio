Composition

Original image on the left, edited on the right. I started my composition off by following along with the lesson--I used the Wide Angle filter to straighten out the fixer upper image, then used a feather brush to mask out the parts of the church image I wanted to hide. From there I used the clone stamp tool to mend the topmost fixer upper window and to also paint another window onto the church. I used curves to adjust the church lighting, then added the Foggy Night color lookup adjustment.
