North Carolina Zoo

This is an ad for the North Carolina Zoo, which is geared towards families with children. I had a hard time deciding what direction to go with this and wound up honing in on the fact that the zoo builds their habitats as realistically as possible. I tried to get the same feel in my ad by fitting the animals into the background. I darkened the brightness on the lion and elephant pictures to make them look more shaded. The font I used is Whiz Bang, which has an interesting, child-like quality to it.
