E-Card

Using the Camera Raw interface, I adjusted my three pictures until they had similar lighting before placing them into my image. Using the pen tool, I cut out and masked the areas I wanted from the car images using the pen tool and a 1-2px feathered selection--the house I left untouched, placed below the other two layers. I used the clone stamp tool to paint in grass, leaves, and extend the roof above the rusted car. I applied the 'Horror Blue' color lookup style to the image. Text styles used are drop shadow and stroke.
