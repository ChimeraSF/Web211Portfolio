Retouch

A simple retouch. I dropped the flower layer down to about 40% opacity so I could see the edges of the ribbon under it. Used the lasso tool with a 2 point feather to circle the ribbon section I wanted, and ctrl+J to make the selection it's own layer. I moved that layer to the top of the list, then raised the flower layer's opacity back to 100%.
