3D Composition

For my 3D object I created a lamp, which I placed into a dining room setting found here. I split the lamp into three parts: the shade, the post, and the stand, then combined all three pieces on one layer. Each piece uses the Bend X 360 shape preset to create the rounded look. I gave the lampshade the Fun Textured material, and both the post and stand pieces the Redwood material. I applied a mask to the lamp so that it was positioned behind the table, then gave it a drop shadow. I applied individual Brightness/Contrast adjustment layers to both the background and the lamp. Lastly, I added the Futuristic Bleak color lookup to the overall image.
